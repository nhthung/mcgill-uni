The Linux Documentation Project (LDP) is a loosely organized team of volunteers that creates and freely distributes online a canonic documentation of the GNU/Linux operating system. Its members strive to create a high-quality, easy to use/search documentation system, and collaborate on all issues concerning said documentation.
